About

I study how machines can perceive and understand the world: depth, geometry, interaction, and vision-language grounding. My work combines classical vision (homography, stitching, geometry) with deep models for perception and control. I'm preparing for graduate research in 3D scene understanding and robotic perception.

Research: 3D reconstruction, depth estimation, multi-camera stitching, robustness in autonomous driving perception.
Also exploring: Persian text-to-speech models that can imitate professional announcers with minimal audible artifacts.
